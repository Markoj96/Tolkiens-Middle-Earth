CINEMA CITY Center started on May 31, 2007.
The atrium center is 1.543 m2 in size. It consists of two independent units. These are the ART Cinema and a shopping mall with 19 outlets of diverse content.
ART Cinema has two cinemas:
• SALA 1 - 169 seats,
• SALA 2 - 68 seats,

which are equipped with luxurious seats of the famous Turkish company UROSAN.

Projection technique from German firm KINOTON and sound system K.C.S. (Kelonik Cinema Sound) provide optimum conditions for the development of cinematic projections.
Dolby surround sound system with:

• SALA 1 - Eight speakers placed on the 3 wall of the cinema and 7 large speakers placed behind a canvas dimension of 7.4 x 3.2 m;
• SALA 2 - four speakers placed on the 3 wall of the cinema and 7 large speakers placed behind the canvas dimensions 6.8 x 3.0m; with a digital sound reader and dolby processors that control everything, provide the highest possible sound quality.
